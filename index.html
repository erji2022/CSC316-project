Climate Change

Global warming is not a distant threat—it is a reality unfolding before our eyes. Driven largely by human-induced emissions and deforestation, the Earth's atmosphere is trapping more heat than ever before.

Over the last century, measurable increases in temperature have reshaped weather patterns, disrupted ecosystems, and threatened human health.

Air Quality

Clean air is essential for healthy living, yet millions of people worldwide face the consequences of polluted skies. Fine particulate matter, especially PM2.5, poses severe health risks and is a direct outcome of industrialization and urban growth.
